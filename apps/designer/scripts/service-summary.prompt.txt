Write a short, clear description of what a [SERVICE NAME] is as a provider offering.

Output must be 3–4 sentences, third person, plain text.

Include:
1) what the service is
2) what types of places it applies to (give a few examples)
3) what the provider typically provides (high‑level deliverables)
4) the outcome/goal

Do NOT describe steps/process/logistics.
Avoid “site visit”, “installation”, “licensed”, “workflow”, “assessment”, “quote”, “scheduling”.

Make each service summary feel uniquely written for that service (vary sentence openings and phrasing; avoid generic filler).
